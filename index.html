What Is Rotoscoping?

Rotoscoping is an animation and visual effects technique where artists trace over live-action footage, frame by frame, to create realistic motion. It helps blend drawn or digital elements with real video, and it is often used for stylized animation, clean mattes, and visual storytelling.
